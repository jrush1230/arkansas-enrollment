Arkansas district map

234 of Arkansas's 235 current-boundary districts are colored by EFA-era enrollment change (green = growth, yellow = near zero, red = decline); click a district to open its detail page, or hover/focus one for a plain-language description of its enrollment trajectory. The 235th, Imboden Charter, has no district boundary of its own and appears instead as an orange point at its school location, alongside Arkansas's other charter schools.
