About the Creator

Joel Rush is an Arkansas-based educator and data analyst. He holds a B.A. in Economics from Bard College and an M.Ed. in Sport Management from the University of Arkansas, and has completed doctoral coursework (ABD) in Education Statistics and Public Policy. He founded The Learning Institute, an Inc. 5000 company, and directed the NPC Makerspace at National Park College. His data and program evaluation work includes engagements with UALR and Children International. He is currently completing Arkansas's iTeach alternative certification program, pursuing secondary licensure in Business Education and Computer Science.
