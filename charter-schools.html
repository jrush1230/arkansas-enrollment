Arkansas Charter Schools

All 22 of Arkansas's public charter school systems (LEAs) with SIS enrollment data, sortable by column except Trajectory (a visual, not a value to rank by). This is a simpler table than the district views on purpose: charters aren't classified by trajectory shape or split into baseline/EFA eras the way districts are; most have far less history to classify from in the first place. The year-over-year and EFA-era change columns cover only the EFA era itself (2022-23 onward), each charter measured from its own first year of data within that era, which for the newest charters is later than 2022-23. One map point is deliberately absent here: Imboden Charter operates as its own school district and gets full treatment in the district views instead.

Trajectory sparklines cover each charter's own full available history: as short as 2 years for a charter that opened in 2024-25, up to all 13 years for the longest-running ones; a short line is the real available window, not an error. The three year-over-year columns each cover one adjacent pair of EFA-era years; a charter that opened mid-era shows "—" in columns predating its own data. Hover or focus a "—" cell for its opening year. EFA-era change is the cumulative percent change from each charter's own first available EFA-era year to its most recent year — unlike the district views' EFA change, which is anchored at 2022-23 for every district uniformly; neither figure is a CAGR. Where a charter has only one year-over-year comparison, its EFA-era change rests on that single pair. Hover or focus the cell for a note when this applies. A mark in the Notes column means the charter operates more than one campus and the enrollment shown is network-wide. Hover or focus the mark for the other campuses.
