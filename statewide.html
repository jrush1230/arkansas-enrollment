Statewide K-12 enrollment, 2013–14 through 2025–26

Fall enrollment in Arkansas's public schools, 2013–14 through 2025–26, from the state's Student Information System (SIS), shown as three series: all student enrollment, public school district enrollment, and public charter school enrollment. Shaded regions mark the baseline era, the COVID enrollment shock, and the EFA (Education Freedom Account) era.

"Other" is the Division of Youth Services School System and the Arkansas School for the Deaf and Blind. "Predecessor" is real school districts later merged into a current district (e.g. Dollarway, absorbed into Pine Bluff in 2021), counted only for their own years before that merger, zero from 2021-22 onward. District + Charter + Other + Predecessor = All, exactly, every year.
